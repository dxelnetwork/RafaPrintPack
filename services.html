Our Services & Capabilities

From dieline engineering to finished pack — three printing technologies with expert packaging design.

Printing Technologies

World-Class Printing

Three complementary technologies for every requirement.

Offset Printing

Best for long-run, color-critical jobs — folding cartons, brochures, magazines, catalogs. Pantone matching and high-fidelity reproduction.

Digital Printing

Ideal for short runs, versioning, market tests. Perfect for business cards, flyers, carton mockups, and variable data applications.

Flexographic Printing

Labels, flexible packaging, and corrugated applications. Durable inks, high-speed production for large-volume runs.

Design & Engineering

Packaging Engineering

Expert structural design and prepress for pharma and CPG.

Custom Structural Design

Dielines tailored to product, shelf, and line constraints. Engineered for optimal material usage.

3D Mockups & Prototypes

Rapid sampling for fit, form, function, and stakeholder sign-off before full production.

Artwork Adaptation

Prepress checks, color management, regulatory panel layout. Proof-to-press alignment for perfect results.

Finishing

Finishing & Converting

Premium finishing to elevate your packaging.

Die-Cutting & Creasing

Precision cutting via LMM MANDELLI 153 for tight tolerances and repeatable multi-up layouts.

Coatings & Lamination

UV varnish, matte/gloss lamination, soft-touch finishes for premium shelf presence.

Emboss, Foil & Special Effects

Emboss/deboss, foil stamping, windowing, spot UV, and tamper-evident features.
