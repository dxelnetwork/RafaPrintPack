The Journey

From Graphic Designer to Founder of Rafa Print Pack

Our Journey Our Facilities
Founder Sirajul Islam Rajon
The Visionary Behind Rafa

My journey began with a deep passion for graphic design — a passion that evolved into a lifelong commitment to the print and packaging industry.

I am Sirajul Islam Rajon. Guided by this unwavering passion, what once started as a creative endeavor has transformed into a dynamic enterprise. We are focused on delivering world-class packaging and print solutions, crafted with precision to meet clients’ high-stakes branding demands.

Managing Director — Rafa Print Pack

Milestones of Excellence

A Journey Built on Passion

1

Building the Foundation

I joined an event management company organizing large-scale events for leading pharmaceutical firms. This experience opened the door to the dynamic world of printing and production.

This early exposure helped me understand not just the principles of design, but how ideas are physically transformed into tangible products.

  • Digital printing
  • Signage & branding
  • Festoons & banners
  • Diverse packaging
2008
Growth
2

From Production to Leadership

Starting as a Production Executive, I spent two years mastering the technical complexities of production. My dedication ultimately led to a promotion as Production Manager, a role I held for three years.

This phase profoundly shaped my leadership mindset and foundational operational expertise.

  • End-to-end production
  • Quality consistency
  • Team supervision
  • Meeting strict deadlines
3

Sales & Marketing Experience

Seeking a broader perspective, I transitioned into Sales & Marketing. Building strong relationships with pharmaceutical and FMCG companies gave me deep insights into specific client needs.

Working closely with clients, I regularly contributed to product development, dummy creation, and modern conceptual designs.

  • Inner & lock cartons
  • Catch covers & pads
  • Metallic printing
  • Syndicated holograms
Expansion
2023
4

The Entrepreneurial Leap

Driven by a clear vision to deliver high-quality, innovative, and deeply reliable solutions, I founded Rafa Print Pack.

Our mission: To combine innovation, precision, and customer satisfaction into every project we undertake.

5

Technology & Excellence

At Rafa Print Pack, we continually invest in advanced machinery and highly efficient production systems. Our LMM MANDELLI 153 Cutting Machine allows us to maintain the strict accuracy and consistency demanded by regulated markets.

Today, our journey is defined by continuous innovation, rigorous quality assurance, and the relationships we hold with our clients.

Present

CEO'S MESSAGE

At Rafa Print Pack, our journey has always been guided by a commitment to precision, innovation, and trust. In the fast-evolving pharmaceutical sector, we understand the responsibility that comes with being a reliable partner in printing and packaging. Our vision is not only to deliver products of the highest quality but also to create lasting value for our clients through sustainable practices, advanced technology, and dedicated service.

We believe success comes from continuous improvement, strong partnerships, and a shared drive for excellence. Together with our passionate team and trusted customers, we look forward to shaping the future of pharmaceutical packaging with integrity, innovation, and reliability.

Sirajul Islam Rajon

Managing Director (MD)
