About Rafa Print Pack

Where quality meets innovation — Dhaka's premier printing and packaging firm specializing in pharmaceutical packaging.

Your Trusted Partner in Printing & Packaging

Rafa Print Pack is a Dhaka-based printing and packaging firm specializing in contract manufacturing for highly regulated industries, most notably pharmaceuticals. We combine offset, digital, and flexographic printing with custom packaging design to deliver market-ready, brand-consistent packaging at scale.

Founded in 2023 with a mandate to raise the quality and responsiveness of local print-packaging, we bring over 17 years of combined industry experience from our leadership team in graphic design, production, and sales within pharmaceutical and print-packaging supply chains.

Legal Name: Rafa Print Pack
Headquarters: 86, Purana Paltan Line, Dhaka 1216, Bangladesh.

Mission & Vision

Core Values

What We Stand For

Process Discipline

SOP-driven prepress, press, and post-press checks ensuring consistent, repeatable quality on every job.

Color Consistency

Press calibration and proof-to-press alignment delivering accurate, vibrant colors across all print runs.

Traceability

Job tickets and batch-level records maintained for regulated customers, ensuring full supply chain transparency.

Innovation

Ongoing investment in equipment, training, and workflow automation to stay at the cutting edge of the industry.

Customer Focus

Dedicated account management, transparent communication, and proactive problem solving at every stage.

Sustainability

Eco-friendly substrates, recyclable designs, and material reduction through structural optimization.

Our Journey

Company Timeline

2008 — 2022

Managing team members gain multi-disciplinary experience in graphic design, production, and sales within pharma events and print-packaging supply chains across Bangladesh.

2023 — Founded

Rafa Print Pack is founded in Dhaka with a mandate to raise the quality and responsiveness of local print-packaging, with a focus on pharmaceutical contract manufacturing.

2024 — Growth & Diversification

Diversification of services (offset, digital, flexo) and deeper focus on regulated industries. Adoption of eco-friendly and luxury finishing options to serve expanding client base.

2025 — Present & Future

Continued investment in precision equipment, quality certifications roadmap (ISO 9001, FSC CoC, GMP), and expansion of sustainable packaging capabilities.
