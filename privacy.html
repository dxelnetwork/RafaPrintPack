Privacy Policy

How we protect your data and maintain industrial confidentiality.

Introduction

At Rafa Print Pack Limited ("we," "us," or "our"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, rafaprintpack.com, or engage with our printing and packaging services.

Information We Collect

We collect information that helps us provide a better service to you, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, work email, phone number, and company details provided via our "Get a Quote" or contact forms.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited.
  • Project Data: Technical specifications and artwork shared during the packaging design and manufacturing process.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used for professional purposes only:

  • To process quotation requests and manage customer accounts.
  • To deliver high-quality printing and packaging services tailored to your brand.
  • To communicate regarding project status, billing, and technical updates.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory pharmaceutical packaging standards.

Data Confidentiality & Security

Given our specialization in Pharmaceutical Packaging, we maintain the highest standards of confidentiality. We implement robust technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access, loss, or theft of your intellectual property and personal information. We do not sell or trade your data to third parties.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze site traffic. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though some site features may become inaccessible.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact our Compliance Officer at info@rafaprintpack.com or visit us at our Dhaka headquarters.
