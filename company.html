Our Group

Concern Companies

A diversified portfolio of companies under the Rafa group, spanning industries from printing to healthcare.

InkPro Print Pack

High-performance packaging solutions including durable corrugated master cartons, precision pharmaceutical blister foils, and high-barrier flexible sachets.

Rafa Paper House

Comprehensive paper trading and distribution — supplying premium-grade paper and board to print houses across Bangladesh.

Rafa Enterprises

Multi-sector trading and business solutions, managing supply chains and commercial operations across diverse industries.

Rafa Bus Travels Ltd.

Inter-city passenger transport with modern, comfortable coach services ensuring safe and reliable travel across the country.

Rafa International School & College

Modern educational institution offering holistic academic programs with a focus on nurturing future leaders and innovators.

Rafa Hospital

Quality healthcare services with skilled medical professionals, advanced diagnostics, and compassionate patient-centered care.

Haidar Ali Memorial Hifz Model Madrasa

A model Islamic educational institution dedicated to Quran memorization and Islamic studies alongside modern academic curriculum.

Rafa Construction Industries

Civil engineering and construction solutions — from residential developments to commercial infrastructure projects.

Sonia Fashion Ltd.

Contemporary fashion design and manufacturing with an emphasis on quality craftsmanship and trending styles for local and export markets.

Sonia Garments Industry

Ready-made garment production for international brands — specializing in knitwear, woven, and compliant manufacturing.

Sonia Farmers and Agro Industry

Sustainable agriculture and farming operations producing fresh produce, poultry, and dairy for local and regional supply chains.

Sonia Pharma Industry

Pharmaceutical manufacturing with GMP-compliant facilities, producing quality medicines and healthcare products for a healthier future.
