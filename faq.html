Our Company

Our main production facility is located in Dhaka, Bangladesh. We are strategically positioned to serve the major pharmaceutical and FMCG manufacturing hubs in the region with efficient logistics and timely delivery.

We specialize in serving highly regulated and quality-sensitive markets, including Pharmaceuticals, FMCG (Fast Moving Consumer Goods), Personal Care, and Corporate sectors. Our processes are designed to meet the specific documentation and compliance needs of each industry.

Founded by Sirajul Islam Rajon, Rafa Print Pack was established to combine technical precision with creative packaging excellence. Built on a foundation of years of industry leadership and a passion for graphic design, we have grown into a premier partner for brands seeking uncompromising quality.

Capabilities & Quality

We offer a comprehensive suite of printing technologies under one roof, including high-speed Offset printing for large commercial runs, flexible Digital printing for shorter runs or variable data, and specialized Flexographic printing for labels,foils, and flexible packaging.

We maintain a "pharma-first" mindset throughout our facility. This includes batch-level traceability, SOP-driven quality control processes, and investments in high-precision machinery like the LMM MANDELLI 153 to ensure the tight tolerances required by regulated markets.

Yes, our facility is equipped with state-of-the-art automated systems and high-capacity presses. We are capable of managing large-scale commercial orders while maintaining strict quality consistency and meeting demanding market lead times.

Products & Support

Our diverse product range includes folding cartons, inner and master cartons, pharmaceutical leaflets, labels, blister foils, and sachets/pouches. We provide end-to-end solutions from dieline design to the finished product.

Absolutely. Our value-added services include dieline optimization, graphic design support, and the creation of physical or 3D mockups. This allows our clients to visualize and test their packaging designs before moving into full-scale production.
