Precision Technology

LMM MANDELLI 153

Our program-controlled cutting machine ensures exact precision with tight tolerances for every carton and label cut. Offering a bed size of 153 cm, it guarantees perfect repeatability for multi-up layouts—a critical requirement for regulated pharmaceutical packaging.
